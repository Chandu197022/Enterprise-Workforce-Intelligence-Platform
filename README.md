# Enterprise Workforce Intelligence & Employee Retention Analytics Platform

## Executive Summary

This project delivers a comprehensive Workforce Intelligence Platform designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce behavior, compensation patterns, employee experience, and retention risks.

The solution combines Python, SQL, Machine Learning, and Power BI to transform workforce data into actionable business intelligence. The platform enables HR leaders and business stakeholders to identify attrition drivers, monitor workforce health, predict employee turnover, and develop data-driven retention strategies.

---

## Business Problem

Employee attrition creates significant challenges for organizations through increased hiring costs, productivity loss, knowledge transfer gaps, and workforce instability.

This project addresses critical workforce questions:

* Which employees are most likely to leave?
* What factors contribute to employee attrition?
* Which departments and job roles experience the highest turnover?
* How do compensation and satisfaction influence retention?
* What workforce segments require proactive retention strategies?
* How can predictive analytics support HR decision-making?

---

* Analyze workforce demographics and employee behavior.

## Analytical Components

### Data Audit

Performed data quality assessment including:

* Missing value analysis
* Duplicate detection
* Data profiling
* Statistical validation

### Exploratory Data Analysis

Conducted workforce analysis covering:

* Attrition trends
* Department performance
* Compensation analysis
* Employee demographics
* Satisfaction metrics
* Retention indicators

### Workforce Risk Engine

Developed a custom risk scoring framework using:

* Overtime
* Compensation level
* Job satisfaction
* Tenure
* Business travel frequency

Risk classifications:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk
* Critical Risk

### Predictive Attrition Modeling

Built a machine learning model to estimate employee turnover probability.

Model outputs include:

* Attrition Probability
* Predicted Attrition Status
* Workforce Risk Indicators
